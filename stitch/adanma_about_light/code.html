The Architect's Vision

Born and raised in Item, Bende LGA, Obia Emenike Chidera's journey began with a fascination for the rugged landscapes and the communal architecture of Abia State. This early exposure to the balance of nature and human ingenuity became the foundation of Adanma.

With over a decade of experience in structural engineering and urban planning, Obia has led Adanma to become a beacon of modern African engineering. His philosophy merges global technical standards with local contextual needs, ensuring every project respects the environment it inhabits.
